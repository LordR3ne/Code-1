The Adventure of Carlos(Sequel of BinaryDungeon)

After a long and tiring adventure in the Binary Dungeon you just want to get as far away from this place as possible

You see a beautiful sunset that hits the water and lets it glisten in a shining orange

There are two options now:

either you Try to go climb down your way over the dangerous cliffs and hopefully get down unharmed

or you Walk a tiring road that leads down to the beach. But it would take hours

or you do nothing
